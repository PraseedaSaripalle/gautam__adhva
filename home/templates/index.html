About Us

Dear Friends, You all belong to Generation Z. Your aspirations are no more static.We appreciate you as an aspiring individual

At the same time, market needs are also rapidly changing. As an progressive educational institution, it is new age responsibility on us to match your aspirations and dynamic market needs.

It requires huge planning from outside and it requires equal cooperation and commitment from your side. While we respect your aspirations, together have to assess and agree on your competencies and customize individual sustainable career path.

There are many graduates, unable to assess their gifted competencies and unable to create a best career path for happy settlement. If you observe few anomalies, One with best IQ level with great academic record who is perfectly fit for GATE/Core Jobs, wrongly selecting CAT/GMAT and landing in sales job which needs more social quotient and emotional coefficient.

One with high risk taking, with great ideas who capable to start his/her own startup, wrongly influenced by societal constraints, settling in ordinary software job.

One with high mathematical intelligence who can write best algorithms, instead of joining product company, wrongly landed at services companies.

One with academically poor background but highly creative, instead of selecting Gaming or Webpage making as his/her career, wrongly influenced by peer pressure, distracted in their lives.

One with foreign language skills and good English accent skill, instead of taking up ITES jobs, taking up wrong choice and losing their language capability. This mismatch story is endless.


Adhva wants put a check to this mismatch. Are you GAME?

Adhva is aiming towards goal for everyone. Adhva works on a philosophy where genuine individual's interest is protected, promoted and prospered. Lets hear our commitment in the form of a short story

A group of children are playing near two railway tracks. One is in use while the other is non-operational. Only one child is playing on the non-operational track where the rest them on the operational track. The train is coming and signal master is besides the same. He should make a quick call whether to change the train's course to the non-operational track and save most of the kids. However, it means the lone child playing on the non-operational track would be sacrificed. Or would he rather let the train go on its way? He is in serious dilemma. Most people might choose to divert the course of the train, and sacrifice the only one child. It is rational decision as most people would make, morally and emotionally. But, that lone kid made a right decision to play at a safe place where as other ignorant friends chose to play though they know that danger exists. Signal master thought a while and decided to let the train go on its original track and protected genuine boy.

Dear Friends, hope you got the mission of Adhva. Do you want to be that genuine boy or girl? Think Once? Ek Bar Socho?

Career Goals

Broadly, Adhva is having 3 verticals- Heads-On, Hands-On and Hearts-On. Each vertical is critical in its function, role in the society and providing equal opportunity to all types of students..

Heads-On

Heads-On vertical caters to the needs of GATE, CA, CORE JOB and GRE,etc where deep theoretical engineering understanding or research orientation is required

Hands-On

Hands-On vertical works towards needs of IT, ITES, KPO, RPO,& Accounts where it caters to the needs of programming skills, domain skills, language skills etc

Hearts-On

Hearts-On vertical broadly caters to the needs of CAT/GMAT, Civil Services & Startup where individual requires leadership capabilities, managing teams, managing risks, financial management, business execution capabilities etc.
